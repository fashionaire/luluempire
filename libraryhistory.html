HISTORY



Visiola community library was founded by three Nigerian Educationists in 2014. They are three industrial women who reads broadly and also author of books.
They resided in a community where educational resources was low, so they decided to come together to open a Library accessible to the people in the community.
The central focus of the library is STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) educational resources.
The library offers facilities to conduct research, study and work in small groups. It has the sitting capacity of one thousand (1000) and the digital services department is made up of 3units namely, information literacy unit; reference and information services unit; and research and catalog unit.

MISSION

The visiola community library provides quality resources and innovative creativity for intellectual curiosity, and to facilitate lifelong learning and reserach within the community we serve.

VISION

The Visiola community library will be the hub and academic center for the community by being an essential component of the academic and creative life of the community. We excel in: Collecting, preserving, and providing access to the best educational resources.
